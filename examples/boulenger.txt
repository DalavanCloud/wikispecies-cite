[[File:Boulenger George 1858-1937.png|thumb|200px|George Albert Boulenger]]
'''George Albert Boulenger''' (September 19, 1858 – November 23, 1937), Belgian-British zoologist. Boulenger described 1,096 species of fish, 556 species of amphibians, and 872 species of reptiles.
